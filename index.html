How to Use

Follow these simple steps to generate professional verification documents in seconds.

  • 1
    Select Document Type Choose the type of document you need from the dropdown menu (e.g., Official Letter, ID Card, Pay Stub).
  • 2
    Enter Details Fill in the form fields manually, or click the "Auto-Fill Random Data" button to populate sample information for testing.
  • 3
    Customize If needed, upload a school logo or your photo (for ID cards). The preview updates in real-time.
  • 4
    Export Once satisfied, use the buttons below the preview to Download PNG, Print/PDF, or Copy Text.
Tip: For the best results when printing to PDF, ensure your browser's print settings have "Background graphics" enabled.

About Us

Welcome to ChatGPTDG (ChatGPT Documents Generator), the premier tool designed exclusively for the educational community.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To empower educators. In an increasingly digital world, teachers often face bureaucratic hurdles. The process of verifying employment or status to access advanced tools like OpenAI's ChatGPT Educator Pilot can be cumbersome and time-consuming.

We believe that verification should not be a barrier to innovation. Teachers should spend their time lesson planning, mentoring, and inspiring students, not fighting with formatting software or waiting weeks for administrative paperwork.

Who We Are

Founded in 2024, ChatGPTDG emerged from a collective of former educators, school administrators, and web developers who saw a gap in the system. We realized that many schools lack the dedicated IT infrastructure to generate on-demand verification documents instantly. Our team came together to build a bridge between educational requirements and modern AI accessibility.

Why Choose Us?

  • Privacy First: Data never leaves your device. We respect your privacy and the confidentiality of your institution's data.
  • Speed & Efficiency: Documents are generated instantly in your browser, saving you days of waiting time.
  • Flexibility: We support multiple standardized formats including Official Letters, ID Cards, Pay Stubs, and Certification Licenses, ensuring you have the right document for every situation.
  • Accessibility: Our tool is free to use and accessible on any modern web browser, with no software installation required.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Welcome to the ChatGPT Documents Generator. Your privacy and data security are critically important to us. This policy outlines how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

We prioritize data minimization. ChatGPTDG is engineered as a client-side tool, meaning the "backend" logic runs entirely within your own web browser.

a. Personal Data

When you input sensitive information such as your name, school address, salary details, or certification numbers, we do not transmit or store this data on our servers. All generation happens locally. Once you close or refresh the tab, this data is wiped from your browser's temporary memory.

b. Uploaded Images

If you upload a school logo, signature, or personal photo, these files are processed locally using the HTML5 FileReader API. They are rendered directly onto the canvas in your browser and are never uploaded to any external cloud storage or third-party server.

2. Data Security

Because we do not database your entry data, the risk of a central data breach from our end is virtually non-existent. However, as the data is displayed on your screen, we recommend using this tool on a secure, private device and avoiding public computers where screen caching might occur.

3. Local Storage and Cookies

We utilize minimal local storage solely to maintain your session interface preferences, such as your "Light/Dark" mode selection. No personal identifiable information (PII) is stored in cookies that persist beyond your current session.

4. Third-Party Tracking

We are committed to a clean, distraction-free user experience. We do not utilize invasive third-party analytics services like Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel to track your behavior. Your usage patterns are not profiled, sold, or shared with advertisers.

5. Legal Disclaimer

This tool is intended for legitimate verification and simulation purposes only (e.g., for authorized educators demonstrating eligibility). Users are responsible for ensuring the accuracy and legality of the information they generate. ChatGPTDG is not responsible for the misuse of generated documents for fraudulent purposes.
